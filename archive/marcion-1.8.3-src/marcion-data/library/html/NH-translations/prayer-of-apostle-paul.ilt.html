The Prayer of the Apostle Paul

Nag Hammadi Library I/I, The Prayer of the Apostle Paul

English Translation by Dieter Mueller
interlinear translation by Milan Konvicka, 13. Nov 2010, latest update: 2. Feb 2011

translation

(1/3)... your light,
(1/3b)give me your mercy!
(1/3c)-(1/6)My Redeemer, redeem me, for I am yours; the one who has come forth from you.
(1/6)You are my mind; bring me forth!
(1/7)You are my treasure house; open for me!
(1/7b)-(1/8)You are my fullness; take me to you!
(1/8)-(1/11)You are (my) repose; give me the perfect thing that cannot be grasped!
(1/11)-(1/14)I invoke you, the one who is and who pre-existed in the name which is exalted above every name, through Jesus Christ, the Lord of Lords, the King of the ages;
(1/15)-(1/18)give me your gifts, of which you do not repent, through the Son of Man, the Spirit, the Paraclete of truth.
(1/18)-(1/19)Give me authority when I ask you;
(1/19)-(1/21)give healing for my body when I ask you through the Evangelist,
(1/22)-(1/23)and redeem my eternal light soul and my spirit.
(1/23)-(1/25)And the First-born of the Pleroma of grace -- reveal him to my mind!
(1/25)-(1/29)Grant what no angel eye has seen and no archon ear (has) heard, and what has not entered into the human heart
(1/30)-(1/33)which came to be angelic and (modelled) after the image of the psychic God when it was formed in the beginning, since I have faith and hope.
(1/35)-(2/2)And place upon me your beloved, elect, and blessed greatness, the First-born, the First-begotten, and the wonderful mystery of your house;
(2/2)-(2/6)for yours is the power and the glory and the praise and the greatness for ever and ever. Amen.
(2/7)-(2/10)Prayer of Paul (the) Apostle. In Peace. Christ is holy.
